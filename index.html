THE PROBLEM

Why Most New Options Traders
Blow Up Their Account

It is almost never bad strategy. It is missing risk management. Three patterns that account for the majority of blown accounts.

PATTERN 01

No Position Sizing

Taking 10% of the account on a single trade. One bad week cuts the portfolio in half. A 50% loss needs a 100% gain just to break even.

PATTERN 02

Moving The Stop

Hard stop set at entry, then moved "just this once" when the trade goes against you. Small manageable losses become account-ending drawdowns.

PATTERN 03

Strategy Hopping

Twenty half-understood strategies instead of four mastered ones. No system survives a full market cycle if you abandon it after two losing trades.
